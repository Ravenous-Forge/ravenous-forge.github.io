About

We are Ravenous Forge presenting Terra Inferno!

Humans have landed on Terra Inferno, spreading like a virus, bringing pollution and robbing the planet of its precious resources. But they have stirred you from your slumber and you are retaliating by bringing the environment to life and causing natural calamities!

In this Turn-Based Tactics meets Resource Management game, spread wildfires across forests to incinerate your foes, conjure up rain storms to extinguish the flames, and move mountains to protect precious areas using the power of the world around you. Use the Florians and the planet to your advantage to destroy the Human's Mothership and save Terra Inferno!

As the planet, spread wildfires across forests to incinerate your foes then divert rivers to simmer the roaring flames and wash away your enemies. Raise and topple mountains to stop the invaders and rid yourself of this plague.
