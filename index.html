About

We are Ravenous Forge presenting Terra Inferno!

Terra Inferno is a Turn-Based Tactics meets Resource Management Game where you play as the Planet, Defending yourself from Humans.

Humans have landed on Terra Inferno, spreading like a virus, bringing pollution and robbing the planet of its precious resources. But they have stirred the planet from its slumber and it's retaliating by bringing the environment to life and causing natural calamities!

As the planet, spread wildfires across forests to incinerate your foes then divert rivers to simmer the roaring flames and wash away your enemies. Raise and topple mountains to stop the invaders and rid yourself of this plague.
